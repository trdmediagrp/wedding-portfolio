Hi Kristen — here's the look I'm proposing for your day.

None of these are your photos. They're frames from a similar shoot I'm using to show you the style, lighting and pacing I'd bring to your wedding day. Think of it as a reference reel — the vibe, not the literal plan.

I haven't seen your dress, florals, or venue setup yet, so the specifics on your day will look different. What carries over is the approach: editorial portraits, soft natural light where possible, real candids mixed with composed frames, and a few signature moments shot intentionally — not in passing.
